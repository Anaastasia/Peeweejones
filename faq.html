How do I order food?

Step 1: Sign up for an Account

Step 2: Press on Restaurants

Step 3: Press on a chosen Restaurant and the chosen food

Step 4: Check out and wait for your order!

Are there any restrictions on ordering?

Nope! Not at all! The restaurants and riders will be happy to serve you!

How long does it take for my food to arrive?

It depends widely on the restaurant and the timing that you order

For example, if you order during lunch time, there might be many orders piling up

and it might take quite some time for the restaurant to process your order

When will the services start and end?

Weekdays: 11am till 9pm

Weekends: 11am till 11pm
